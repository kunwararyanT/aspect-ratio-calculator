About RatioCraft

RatioCraft is a zero-bloat pixel and aspect ratio calculation utility designed specifically for video editors, motion designers, and content creators. Calculating custom render resolutions for platforms like Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and Facebook can be tedious when aiming to prevent aspect distortion.

Built to fit seamlessly into high-performance editing workflows (DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, After Effects), RatioCraft allows creators to instantly scale horizontal and vertical pixel dimensions while maintaining exact geometric proportions.

Client-Side & Private

We respect your privacy. RatioCraft operates 100% locally in your web browser. No project specs or custom dimension data are ever recorded or transferred to external servers.
