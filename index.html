What is the Instagram Reels Aspect Ratio?

The official Instagram Reels aspect ratio is 9:16. The ideal resolution for exporting Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and TikTok clips is 1080 x 1920 pixels. Maintaining a 9:16 vertical frame ensures your media displays full-screen on mobile devices without black bars or accidental cropping.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate pixel aspect ratio without losing quality?

To rescale video dimensions without stretching, divide your target width by the original aspect ratio factor or use our automated calculator above to lock the proportion automatically.

What happens if I upload 16:9 video to Instagram Reels?

Instagram will add thick black bars above and below a 16:9 widescreen video to fit it into the vertical player. For best engagement, re-crop your timeline to 9:16 (1080x1920) in editing software like DaVinci Resolve or Premiere Pro.
